Affordable Housing in San Diego

Identifying Housing Needs Through Machine Learning

Using a KMeans clustering machine learning model on income and housing cost data we are able to make predictions
to suggest which areas of San Diego would benefit from high-density low-income housing. After identifying these areas we
investigate which areas have the greatest amenities such as close proximity to schools, hospitals, supermarkets, and parks.
